# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

• Never approve unknown UPI collect requests  
• Do not share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone  
• Verify payment requests before approving  
• Avoid clicking suspicious links or messages  
• Use strong passwords for payment apps  
• Enable two-factor authentication  
• Regularly check bank transaction history  
• Immediately report suspicious activity
